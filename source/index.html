We are a group of makers and hackers who are putting together a hackerspace on the south side of Chicago (in the Bridgeport/Pilsen area, where most of us live). We are seeking hackers, tinkerers, hobbyists, crafters, artists and doers to help us create a social, educational and collaborative space for ourselves and the community.

If you have any questions, email us. If you have no questions, join us!

We're currently open for hacking! If you're interested, feel free to stop by our regular meetings, Wednesdays at 7:30!
